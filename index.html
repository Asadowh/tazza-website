Tazza Restaurant Favorites

HUMMUS

Puree of chick peas, tahini paste, and lemon juice topped with whole chick peas, garlic, and olive oil. Served with two pita bread and pickles.

BABA GHANOUSH

Puree of charbroiled eggplant, tahini, dry mint, and lemon juice topped with house salad, parsley, and olive oil. Served with two pita bread and pickles.

LABANEH

Creamy dip made from strained yogurt topped with dry mint and olive oil. Served with two pita bread and pickles.

FOULE (FAVA BEANS)

Petra foule is puree of fava beans, garlic, lemon and jalapeno topped with whole chick peas, olive oil, and parsley. Served with two pita bread and pickles.

FALAFEL PLATE

Eight fried patties of ground chick peas, onions, herbs, and parsley served with tahini salad. Served with two pita bread and pickles.

FRIED KIBBE

Ground patties stuffed with beef, onions, pine nuts, wheat crumbs, and herbs. Served with two pita bread and pickles.

GRAPE LEAVES

Ten pieces. Grape leaves stuffed with rice, tomatoes, onions, parsley, olive oil, herbs, and lemon juice. Served with two pita bread and pickles.

NABULSI FRIED CHEESE

Six pieces. White cheese pan fried in olive oil. Served with two pita bread and pickles.
